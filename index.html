TORORO

Tororo is a town in Eastern Uganda, located near the border with Kenya. It is known for its rich cultural heritage and vibrant community. The town is surrounded by beautiful landscapes, including hills and valleys, making it a picturesque destination for visitors. Tororo is also home to various markets where locals sell their goods, providing a glimpse into the daily life of the residents. The town has a warm and welcoming atmosphere, making it a great place to experience Ugandan culture and hospitality.
