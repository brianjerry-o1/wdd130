Task 3: Nesting Block in Inline

I am a span (inline)
I am a div (block) nested inside a span.

Issue: This is "invalid HTML." While the browser might try to render it, it often "breaks" the span into two pieces or forces the div to a new line, disrupting the flow. Why? Structurally, block elements are "containers" while inline elements are "content." You shouldn't put a container inside a piece of content.
